Fill as many lines at the bottom as possible. Use UP and DOWN buttons to walk left/right and to push crates. To jump use SELECT. To use super jump press SELECT twice. Jump against a falling crate to break it. Collect powerups to save you from the crates. Watch out for exploding bomb crates! They destroy everything that surrounds them. Connect similar crates together in horizontal or vertical lines of 3 or more to make them disappear. Watch out for bonuses that open secret characters!
